HOTKEYS:

Space
Toggle play pause.
Right Arrow
Skip to next song.
Left click on Artist
Show the Albums of the Artist.
Left click on Album
Show the Tracks of the Album.
Left Double Click on Artist/Album/Track
Queue the Artist/Album/Track and begin to play if not already playing.
Right Click on Artist/Album/Track
Cache the Artist/Album/Track, this saves the files locally so that you can play them whitout internet connection. Cached items are marked with a different color in the listings.
Left click on item in the Queue
Select the item in the queue.
Delete
Removes the selected songs from the queue.
Shift+Delete
Clears the whole queue.

Tecnical Details:

When a song is cached it's marked instantaniously, but that doesn't mean that it's downloaded yet. The browser will begin to download the songs at once, saving them in the application cache using the html5 file-API. If you shut down the app while it's downloading songs, it will continue where it left when you turn it back on (given that you have a internet connection.) Currently there is no way of seeing which tracks are already downloaded. This could lead to funky behaviour if you set a lot of tracks for caching. Shut down the app before it's done downloading. If you don't have an internet connection when you turn the app back on, it will still show the tracks that you have marked for caching but not downloaded yet. Trying to play them will fail, but should not crash the application.
